About

From a LUMS classroom project to a national data platform

I'm a software engineer and open-data specialist with 5+ years of experience building national-scale portals and enterprise web applications. I started with a final-year university project modernizing Pakistan's first CKAN-based open data portal, then spent two years at Rolustech building enterprise Angular systems for a US-based logistics platform, and most recently led the CKAN-based National Open Government Data Portal for Jordan's Ministry of Digital Economy & Entrepreneurship — covering 170+ ministries and 4,000+ datasets.

Outside of client work, I mentor teams on clean code and system design, run coding sessions, and build self-directed automation projects to keep learning by shipping.
